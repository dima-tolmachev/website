# Welcome to the Front-end Engineer Handbook

This comprehensive guide will help you master the skills needed to become a world-class front-end engineer. Whether you're just starting out or looking to level up your career, this handbook covers everything you need to know.

## What You'll Learn

- **JavaScript Fundamentals** - Deep dive into the language that powers the web
- **React Mastery** - Build scalable applications with modern React patterns
- **TypeScript** - Type-safe development for maintainable code
- **Performance** - Make your applications blazing fast
- **System Design** - Architect front-end systems at scale
- **Career Growth** - Navigate your path to senior and beyond

## Who Is This For?

This handbook is designed for:

- Junior developers looking to build a strong foundation
- Mid-level engineers wanting to reach senior level
- Senior engineers preparing for staff+ roles
- Anyone passionate about front-end excellence

Let's begin your journey to front-end mastery.
